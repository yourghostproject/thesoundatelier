I — The Concept

Every great love story
deserves its own soundtrack.

The Sound Atelier crafts two hours of original, bespoke music, composed exclusively for your day. From smooth cocktail grooves to refined dinner soundtracks, every note is written to reflect you — your style, your story, your rhythm.

A hand-picked selection is pressed onto custom-designed vinyl, complete with bespoke artwork by our lead designer. On the day, your guests leave with their own copy — a memento of the celebration and the music that made it unforgettable.

Music made for moments,
not for everyone.

II — The Atelier

As Sony Music roster producers with thousands of physical sales worldwide, our team collaborates with celebrated songwriters and artists across Los Angeles, Seoul, London and beyond — bringing global artistry into life's most personal moments.

The Sound Atelier introduces the first on-demand bespoke music service for private events and brand commissions: tailor-made tracks for every chapter of a celebration, paired with a hand-pressed vinyl artefact designed to outlast the day.

III — The Process
Step 01

Discover

We begin with a personal creative consultation — a conversation to understand your story, mood, and aesthetic. Together we explore the atmosphere you want to create and the feeling you want your guests to remember.

Step 02

Compose

Our producers craft a two-hour bespoke soundtrack. Each piece is written and mixed to reflect your style, your rhythm, and your story — from welcome to last dance.

Step 03

Press

A curated 40-minute selection is pressed onto luxury vinyl, with bespoke sleeve artwork tailored to your aesthetic. A record that looks as beautiful as it sounds.

Five design directions.
One for your story.

IV — The Keepsake

Every sleeve is hand-designed by our lead designer Stuart Tolley, printed in CMYK with matt lamination and finished with metallic foils — red, gold, black or white — to match the spirit of the music inside.
