II - Case Studies
Case One

Lake Como Three-Day Wedding

Newly engaged couple planning their wedding with a top destination wedding planner.
The Brief

A three-day wedding on Lake Como for an American couple who were welcoming friends and family from around the world. The music brief was to set the tone for the weekend during the welcome drinks reception - elegant & romantic with a contemporary edge. The atmosphere is to be effortless and full of life, immediately making every guest feel at ease, welcomed, connected and excited for the celebrations ahead.

The Sound

In our studio, our writers and producers composed and produced a bespoke soundtrack designed exclusively for the occasion. Deep house grooves were layered with sweeping string arrangements, blending contemporary electronic rhythms with cinematic orchestration. The result was a sound that felt elegant, romantic and unmistakably modern, setting the perfect tone for the weekend ahead.

The Keepsake

100 bespoke vinyl records, each housed in a custom-designed sleeve inspired by the colours of a Lake Como sunset. Each record arrived with hand-set typography featuring the couple's names and wedding date. Presented to guests over breakfast the following morning, the soundtrack became a tangible memento of the first evening - not just a piece of music, but a beautifully crafted piece of art that captured the feeling of the weekend's unforgettable beginning.

Case Two

Client Dinner Cruise

A wealth management firm hosting their most valued clients. Private dinner on the Thames, London.
The Brief

A leading wealth management firm wanted to host its most valued clients for an evening that felt different from the traditional corporate dinner. The ambition was to create an atmosphere that was contemporary, relaxed and culturally current, where conversations unfolded naturally, relationships deepened organically, and guests left feeling part of something memorable rather than simply attending another dinner event.

The Sound

In our studio, our writers and producers created music inspired by the warmth and rhythm of Latin America. Contemporary Brazilian percussion, Afro-house grooves, nu-disco, and soulful electronic textures provided a sophisticated backdrop that gradually evolved throughout the evening. The result was a soundtrack that encouraged movement without demanding attention, lifting the energy of the room, sparking conversation and seamlessly transforming a formal dinner into a vibrant social experience.

The Keepsake

150 numbered vinyl records, housed in matte-black sleeves featuring a subtle foil-embossed Thames motif and the firm's initials. More akin to a limited-edition art piece than traditional corporate merchandise, each record became a lasting reminder of the evening - an object designed to be displayed, played and treasured long after the final course had been served.

Case Three

Immersive World-Building

A tech studio launching a fantasy video game after four years in development. Immersive brand activation.
The Brief

Bring the game's universe to life. Guests weren't there to sample a product; they were there to step inside an entirely new world.

The Sound

Working alongside the game's concept artist and visual director, we composed a cinematic score that evolved throughout the activation. Orchestral themes, atmospheric textures, ancient otherworldly textures and moments of carefully timed tension and release transformed every space into part of the narrative.

The Keepsake

500 limited-edition vinyl records featuring bespoke game artwork and a bespoke score composed exclusively for the activation. A collector's piece rather than a promotional item, each record allowed guests to own a tangible piece of the universe before it officially came to life.

III - The Process
Step 01

Discover

We begin with a personal creative consultation - a conversation to understand your story, mood, and aesthetic. Together we explore the atmosphere you want to create and the feeling you want your guests to remember.

Step 02

Compose

Our producers craft a two-hour bespoke soundtrack. Each piece is written and mixed to reflect your style, your rhythm, and your story - from welcome to last dance.

Step 03

Press

A curated 40-minute selection is pressed onto luxury vinyl, with bespoke sleeve artwork tailored to your aesthetic. A record that looks as beautiful as it sounds.
